🔨 Making It Right — Recovering a Missed Mark

A miss isn’t always the end of the month. Where the value can still be recovered, you can make it right and earn partial credit back. Where the value is time-sensitive and permanently lost — documentation that had to be captured on site, an estimate that had to go out same-day, a no-call/no-show that left the team short — it can’t be made up. That’s the line: recoverable value can be earned back; lost value can’t.

✓ Can be made up → restores to Partial ($50)
EOD after 6pm — submit a complete EOD by the next morning.
Reworks — correct the issue promptly and document the fix.
Missed water tag — tag upcoming eligible jobs to catch up.
Missed drop-offs — schedule additional breakfasts to make up the difference.
Attendance — out sick only — pick up an after-hours loss when not on call and extra help is needed.
Van reset — stay late or come in Saturday to organize the warehouse and/or clean & decontaminate equipment. → restores to full ($100)
✕ Can’t be made up
Attendance — no-call/no-show — leaves the team short with no notice; can’t be recovered.
Jobsite documentation — photos, readings, notes, signed forms must be captured on site; it’s imperative to substantiate invoices.
Estimate / invoice turnaround — same-day estimates and 24-hour invoices; the timing is the value.
80% estimate conversion — a monthly threshold; it’s met or it isn’t.
Collections minimum — the monthly target must be met; no makeup.

A successful makeup restores the metric to Partial ($50) — it credits the recovery while keeping a clean, first-time Met ($100) meaningful. The Van Reset is the one exception: a full Saturday of warehouse organization / equipment decontamination restores the full $100, reflecting the real extra effort. Makeups are confirmed by management.

How metrics earn:  Building metrics (jobsite docs, EOD, water tags, breakfasts, drop-offs) start at $0 and build up as you do them — an open dot means “in progress, still earning.”  Clean-record metrics (attendance, no reworks, van reset) start the month on track for the full amount and stay green as long as the record stays clean. This way nothing rewards an unwanted event, and the open dots early in the month simply mean the month is still young.
